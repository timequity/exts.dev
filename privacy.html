Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

exts.dev is a collection of browser extensions created by 0x7669. We believe your browsing activity is none of our business. All our extensions are designed with privacy as a core principle.

Common Principles

All extensions from exts.dev follow these principles:

  • No tracking: We don't collect analytics, usage data, or browsing history
  • No accounts: No sign-up, no login, no user profiles
  • Local storage only: All settings are stored locally on your device
  • No network requests: Extensions don't phone home or send data anywhere
  • Open source: All code is publicly auditable on GitHub

GrayMode

Third-Party Services

Our extensions don't integrate with any third-party services. The exts.dev website uses Cloudflare for hosting, which may collect anonymous analytics as described in their privacy policy.
